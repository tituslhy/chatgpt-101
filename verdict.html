The Verdict

⚡ ChatGPT
The best AI for the most people. Images, voice, video, generous free tier, vague-prompt wizardry. The armory with a weapon for every occasion. Also the only one that can draw, sing, and generate a 5-second clip of your dog as an astronaut — all at S$27/month.
🔶 Claude
The best AI for people who take their work seriously. Writes better, codes better, pushes back when you're wrong, wins 70% of enterprise deals while ChatGPT wins the market share conversation. In the right hands, for the right job, nothing else comes close. Also S$27/month. Genuinely insane value.

The Bottom Line

ChatGPT is not a chatbot. It hasn't been one for two years. It started as an accidental revolution, survived a boardroom coup, renegotiated a $13B exclusive deal the moment it was big enough to, and launched the world's fastest-growing mobile app in history.

It runs on a FastAPI monolith its own engineers describe as "a bit of a dumping ground."

And at S$27/month, it remains one of the two most underpriced products in the history of software.

The other one costs S$27/month too. You know which one.
